Engineer

Defense
  • Health: 125 HP
  • Abilities: Build Sentry, Dispenser, Teleporter
  • Primary Weapon: Shotgun
Meet the Engineer (Video)

Overview

The Engineer is a support class who excels at building and maintaining machines to help his team. His sentry guns provide powerful area defense, while dispensers and teleporters keep teammates supplied and mobile.

Background

The Engineer is a soft-spoken Texan with a knack for mechanics and invention. He is always ready to lend a hand and keep his team's infrastructure running smoothly.

Tips & Strategy

  • Place sentry guns in strategic locations to defend objectives.
  • Keep dispensers and teleporters upgraded for maximum team support.
  • Use your shotgun for personal defense when machines are under attack.

Trivia

  • The Engineer is voiced by Grant Goodeve.
  • He can upgrade and repair his buildings using his wrench.
  • His southern drawl and friendly attitude make him a fan favorite.
