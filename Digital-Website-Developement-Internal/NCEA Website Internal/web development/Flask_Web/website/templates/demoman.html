Meet the Demoman (Video)

Overview

The Demoman is an expert in explosives, capable of laying traps and controlling key areas with sticky bombs and grenades. He excels at both offense and defense, making him a versatile choice for any team.

Background

The Demoman is a one-eyed Scottish demolitions expert, known for his love of explosions and strong accent. He is both feared and respected for his destructive capabilities.

Tips & Strategy

  • Use sticky bombs to defend objectives and control choke points.
  • Detonate traps when enemies approach for maximum impact.
  • Grenade launcher is effective for indirect fire and area denial.

Trivia

  • The Demoman is voiced by Gary Schwartz.
  • He is the only class with a grenade launcher and sticky bomb launcher.
  • His missing eye is a running joke in TF2 lore.
