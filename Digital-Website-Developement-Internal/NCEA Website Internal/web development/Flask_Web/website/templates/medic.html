Medic

Support
  • Health: 150 HP
  • Abilities: Healing, ÜberCharge
  • Primary Weapon: Medigun
Meet the Medic (Video)

Overview

The Medic is the backbone of any successful team, providing healing and powerful ÜberCharges to keep teammates alive and push objectives.

Background

The Medic is a German doctor with questionable ethics and a love for experimental medicine. His healing abilities are unmatched, and his ÜberCharge can turn the tide of battle.

Tips & Strategy

  • Stay behind teammates and heal them to build ÜberCharge.
  • Use ÜberCharge strategically to break through enemy defenses.
  • Keep moving to avoid being targeted by enemy Spies and Snipers.

Trivia

  • The Medic is voiced by Robin Atkin Downes.
  • He does not have a medical license.
  • His Medigun can overheal teammates beyond their normal health.
