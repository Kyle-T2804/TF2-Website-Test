Meet the Sniper (Video)

Overview

The Sniper excels at eliminating key targets from a distance, controlling open areas and picking off enemies with precision headshots.

Background

The Sniper is an Australian marksman with a calm demeanor and a sharp eye. He prefers to stay out of the fray and take out enemies before they know what hit them.

Tips & Strategy

  • Stay in elevated or hidden positions for better sightlines.
  • Prioritize enemy Medics and Heavies for maximum impact.
  • Watch for enemy Spies who may try to sneak up on you.

Trivia

  • The Sniper is voiced by John Patrick Lowrie.
  • He carries a kukri for close encounters.
  • His jarate can douse enemies and reveal Spies.
