Meet the Heavy (Video)

Overview

The Heavy is the strongest and most resilient class in Team Fortress 2, capable of absorbing massive amounts of damage and dealing it back with his minigun. He is the backbone of any defensive lineup.

Background

Hailing from Russia, the Heavy is known for his love of sandwiches, his deep voice, and his devotion to his weapon, Sasha. He is both feared and respected on the battlefield.

Tips & Strategy

  • Pair with a Medic for maximum survivability and offensive power.
  • Use your high health to protect teammates and hold choke points.
  • Spin up your minigun before rounding corners to surprise enemies.

Trivia

  • The Heavy is voiced by Gary Schwartz.
  • He has the highest base health of any class in TF2.
  • His sandwich can heal both himself and teammates.
