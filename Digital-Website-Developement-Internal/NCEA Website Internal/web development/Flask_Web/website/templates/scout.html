Meet the Scout

Overview

The Scout is the fastest class in Team Fortress 2, excelling at flanking, harassment, and rapid objective captures. His double jump makes him unpredictable and able to reach otherwise inaccessible spots.

Tips & Strategy

Use speed and double jump to approach enemies from unexpected angles.

Capture points twice as fast as other classes—be the first on the scene.

Direct fights with Heavies or sentries are a bad idea—focus on harassment.

Trivia
