Pyro

Offense
  • Health: 175 HP
  • Abilities: Airblast, Afterburn
  • Primary Weapon: Flamethrower
Meet the Pyro (Video)

Overview

The Pyro is a close-range specialist who uses a flamethrower to set enemies on fire and disrupt enemy formations. Pyro excels at area denial and ambushing foes.

Background

The Pyro's identity is shrouded in mystery, with their gender and nationality unknown. Their obsession with fire is legendary, and their view of the world is... unique.

Tips & Strategy

  • Use the airblast to reflect projectiles and extinguish burning teammates.
  • Ambush enemies around corners for maximum damage.
  • Control choke points and defend objectives with fire.

Trivia

  • The Pyro's voice is provided by Dennis Bateman.
  • Pyrovision goggles change the game's visuals for the Pyro.
  • The Pyro's gender is intentionally left ambiguous by Valve.
