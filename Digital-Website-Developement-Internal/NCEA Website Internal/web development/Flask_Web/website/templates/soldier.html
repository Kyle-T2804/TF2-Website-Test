Meet the Soldier (Video)

Overview

The Soldier is one of the most versatile classes in Team Fortress 2, known for his powerful rocket launcher and ability to rocket jump to reach high places and surprise enemies. His balanced stats make him effective in both offense and defense.

Background

The Soldier is a patriotic American who believes discipline and firepower win battles. He is famous for his helmet, shovel, and explosive personality.

Tips & Strategy

  • Master rocket jumping to access strategic locations and evade enemies.
  • Use splash damage to hit multiple opponents or enemies behind cover.
  • Pair with a Medic for devastating pushes using the ÜberCharge.

Trivia

  • The Soldier is voiced by Rick May.
  • He is one of the few classes who can rocket jump.
  • His catchphrase "If fighting is sure to result in victory, then you must fight!" is iconic.
