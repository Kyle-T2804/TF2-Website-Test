Meet the Spy (Video)

Overview

The Spy is a master of deception, able to disguise himself as the enemy and use his cloaking device to infiltrate and eliminate high-value targets.

Background

The Spy is a suave Frenchman, skilled in espionage and subterfuge. He excels at disrupting enemy defenses and picking off key targets.

Tips & Strategy

  • Use disguise and cloak to infiltrate enemy lines undetected.
  • Prioritize backstabbing enemy Medics, Engineers, and Snipers.
  • Sap enemy buildings before attacking Engineers.

Trivia

  • The Spy is voiced by Dennis Bateman.
  • He can disguise as any enemy class.
  • His sapper disables enemy Engineer buildings.
